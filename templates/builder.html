🚀 Deploy your server to Railway

Railway hosts your server.py for free so your Android phone can reach it anywhere on the internet. Click the button below to deploy in one click — no credit card needed.

Deploy to Railway

How to find your URL after deploying

  • 1
    Click Deploy to Railway above. Sign in with GitHub if prompted.
  • 2
    Railway shows a template page called "resourceful-possibility" — click the purple Deploy button on that page to start the deployment.
  • 3
    Wait for the build to finish (green tick ✓) — takes about 1 minute.
  • 4
    Click the service box, open the Settings tab, scroll to Networking → Public Networking and click Generate Domain.
  • 5
    Copy the URL — it looks like https://yourapp-xxxx.up.railway.app — then come back here.
💡 Your Railway URL will look like:
https://nexussmsbridgeserver-production-xxxx.up.railway.app
